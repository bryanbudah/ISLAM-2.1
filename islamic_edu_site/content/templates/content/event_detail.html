About This Event

📖

Join us for a transformative journey into the language of the Quran. This study group is designed for both beginners and those with some background in Arabic who wish to deepen their understanding of the Holy Quran.

Through this event, you will learn how to read and understand Quranic Arabic, explore the linguistic miracles of the Quran, and connect more deeply with the words of Allah. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the fundamentals of Arabic grammar and vocabulary specifically focused on Quranic texts.

This event is part of our ongoing initiative to make Quranic knowledge accessible to everyone in our community. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refresh your knowledge, you'll find value in this session.

✍️

Event Details

✍️

The session will include:

  • Basic Arabic grammar principles
  • Vocabulary building focused on common Quranic words
  • Reading practice with selected Quranic verses
  • Group discussions and Q&A session
  • Resources for continued learning

Please bring a notebook and pen for taking notes. Quran copies and learning materials will be provided. The event is free of charge and open to all community members.

Children above the age of 12 are welcome when accompanied by an adult. Please inform us of any special requirements when registering.
