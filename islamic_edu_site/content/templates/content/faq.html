Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is IlmHub?

IlmHub is an online Islamic educational platform offering structured courses, articles, Quran quotes, and community events to help learners increase their knowledge.

Do I need to create an account to use the site?

You can browse courses, articles, and Quran quotes without an account.

Are the courses free?

Yes, most courses are free. The platform focuses on accessible Islamic learning.

Who creates the content?

All content is curated by trusted admins and educators. Users cannot upload content but can interact through comments and discussions.

What is the Event section?

The Event section lists events, discussions, and activities for online and offline participation.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your account information is stored securely. Passwords are encrypted, and best practices for user authentication are followed.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.

How do I contact support?

Reach out via the Contact Us form below the Home page or at
illhubacademy@gmail.com.
